63/// QR factorization. The upper triangular part of D contains the R factor. The Householder vectors
64/// are stored in the strict lower triangular part of D.
65/// The Householder coefficients are stored in W, which should either be a vector of `A.cols()`
66/// elements, or a matrix of size `geqrf_W_size(A)`. If W has zero rows, the coefficients are
67/// discarded.

72 simdify(W));
